Journeys, Connections, Links

As interaction designers we create and build connections between people, objects, technology, society. But connections are also essential to how we design – we bridge old theories with new, adapt to ever-changing technologies and tools, and draw from multiple disciplines in our day-to-day.

As Interaction takes its own journey to Europe for the first time, come learn from, be inspired by and connect with the global interaction design community. We can’t wait to see you in February.
