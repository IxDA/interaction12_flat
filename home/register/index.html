Before you embark on this form…

  • The online registration system will allow you to: book your conference registration, add optional workshops to your registration, and, optionally, reserve your hotel accommodations as well.
  • After you book we'll give you an access key so you can come back and add workshops to your booking, plus activities and accommodation (for more information about accommodation, check out Where to Stay on our Location page).
  • You can pay by credit card when you book (sorry no Diners) or by invoice (must be paid within 14 days).
  • All prices are in Euro (€).

If you have any questions, or if you are having difficulty accessing or using our system, please send an email to register@interaction.ixda.org.

Day Passes

We are now offering single-day passes for Thursday through Saturday.

Day passes permit access to the conference for one day only at the CCD between 9:00am and 5:30pm.

Day passes do not include access to the evening events, and they are not eligible for refunds, changes, or transfers.

Your conference registration includes:

  • Access to all conference sessions from February 2-4 in the Convention Centre Dublin (CCD)
  • Lunch each day

  • Entry to the middle party on February 2
  • Entry to the Interaction Awards on February 3
  • Entry to the closing party on February 4

Cancellation policies

Conference registration, workshops and activities

Cancellation requests received before 23:59pm (GMT +0:00) on December 31 will receive a 75% refund. Any requests made after this will not be eligible for a refund however you can transfer your registration to another person at no cost.

Requests can be made by emailing your full name and registration number to register@interaction.ixda.org. If you are requesting a transfer please include the full name and contact details (email and phone if possible) of the person you wish to transfer your registration to.

Hotels

The one-night deposit for hotels is non-refundable, so choose wisely!
